Eye Care + Wound Care

One product. Two critical jobs.

iRide® is designed as an eye and face cleaner first — but the same sterile water works as a wound cleaner the moment a ride goes wrong. Two must-have rider products, one can.

Primary Use

Eye & Face Cleaner

Wind, dust, bugs, and grit evaporate the tear film in minutes at highway speed. One fine mist restores it — no burn, no blur, safe for contact-lens wearers.

  • Instant dry-eye relief after miles in the wind
  • Flushes allergens, pollen, and road dust
  • Doubles as a face rinse for sweat and sunblock
  • Works helmet-on through a cracked visor
Secondary Use

Wound Cleaner

When the unexpected happens, the same sterile, preservative-free water flushes road rash, cuts, and abrasions. Immediate self-treatment at the scene — before EMS arrives.

  • Flushes debris from road rash and cuts
  • No sting, no chemicals, no contamination risk
  • Fine mist reaches awkward angles on skin
  • Same can your eyes trust, for wounds too
